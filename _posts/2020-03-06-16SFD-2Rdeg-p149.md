# 16SF/D, 16S2R-degenerate Primer (Fish-16S), amplify **200 pb**, Deagle et.al.2007
## **PCR #1, Pag 149, 03 05 2020**

### **PCR reaction**

-   ~ 30  ng/ul DNA
-   0.5   ul primer (10um)
-   6.25     ul Hoststart **Qiagen**
-   12.5  ul total reaction
-   **0.5  ul BSA**

### **Cycling conditions (Thomsen et al. 2012)**
- 10 min to 94°C
- 35 cycles:
  94°C 30 s,
  **54°C** 30 s,
  72°C 45 s,
- 2 min to 72°C
- 4°C forever
