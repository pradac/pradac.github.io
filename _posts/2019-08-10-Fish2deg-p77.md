# Fish2degCBL, Fish2CBR Primer (cytb-Fish), amplify **80 pb**, Thomsen et al. 2012
## **PCR #4, Pag 77, Aug 10 2019**


### **PCR reaction**

-   ~40 ng/ul DNA
-   1 ul primer (10um)
-   Hoststart **Sahara (new)** 10 ul
-   **1.5 ul** BSA
-   25 ul total reaction


### **Cycling conditions (Thomsen et al. 2012)**
- 7 min to 95°C
- 50 cycles:
  94°C 30 s,
  **50°C** 30 s,
  72°C 20 s,
- 5 min to 72°C
- 4°C forever
